What Do I Do?

I will draw your own character(s) or other request(s) as digital art in semirealism style like the samples above.
I will use Clip Studio Paint to draw.

What Will You Get in Return?

In this illustration service, the illustration product I offers are all in digital files at the moment. You will get high resolution picture(s) in png format. The size will be depended on your order. It can be in A4 size of canvas, A5, or 1440p width, but the size minimum I use for a single headshot picture of a character is 2000x2000 px 350 dpi.
I will also give you a free file source for every order. I will pack all the product into a zip file format and send them to you via email or google drive.

What Illustration Product Do I Offer?

I have three packages to offer you. Figure(s) commission, scene(s) commission, and Reff-Sheet(s) commission.
